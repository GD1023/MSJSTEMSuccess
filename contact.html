Quick answers

Before you email us

Is tutoring free?

Yes! MSJ STEM Success is a free program run by MSJ students for MSJ students.

How long until I'm matched?

Usually a few weeks. If every tutor slot is full, you'll be placed on a waitlist and notified when one opens.

Can I request a specific subject?

Yes! list it on the sign-up form. We match based on subject need and tutor availability.

I'm not an MSJ student. Can I still apply to tutor?

Tutoring runs in MSJ classrooms under MSJ teacher supervision, so the program is open to MSJ students.
