How it works

Built around one relationship, not one homework set.

Every pairing runs on the same rhythm: sign up, get matched, meet consistently, keep meeting. Tutors are trained and experienced Warriors who've already done well in the course they're teaching.

See the full student process →

  1. Sign up online

    Tell us the subject(s) you need help in.

  2. Get matched

    A qualified tutor reaches out within a few weeks — sooner if a spot is open.

  3. Meet consistently

    You and your tutor pick a lunch or after-school slot, at least twice a month.

  4. Stick with it

    Most pairs work together a full semester or longer — that's where the gains show up.

Why it's different

Not a homework hotline.
