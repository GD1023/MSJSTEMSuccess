Here at MSJ STEM Success, we pair students with trained peer tutors for a full school year. We focus on providing instruction that teaches concepts rather than just solving homework questions. We're free, student-run, and open to any math or science course from college prep algebra to multivariable calculus.

How it works

We follow a systematic pairirng process. Sign up, get matched, and meet consistently. Tutors are trained and have already done well in the course they're teaching.

See the full student process →

  1. Sign up online

    Tell us the subject(s) you need help in.

  2. Get matched

    A qualified tutor will reach out within a week, if not sooner.

  3. Meet consistently

    You and your tutor can decide on a after-school slot. We suggest meeting at least twice a month.

  4. Stick with it

    Most pairs work together a full semester or longer. That's where the gains truly show up.

Why it's different

We are not just a homework hotline.

01 · DEPTH

Concept over cram (yes, even at our CRAM sessions!)

We aim for real understanding of a subject, not a rushed fix for upcoming assignment.

02 · CONTINUITY

Consistency

Meeting for longer periods can help tutors actually learn how you think and better assist you.

03 · TRUST

Peer-taught, teacher-supervised

All sessions run in MSJ classrooms under teacher supervision to ensure safe spaces and real accountability.
