About the program

Progress Together.

MSJ STEM Success is the peer tutoring and enrichment arm of the Mission San Jose High School

Math and science department — run for students, by students, since 2016.

Our mission

Understanding, not just answers.

We pair students and tutors together for a school year whith one goal: building real concept development and understanding. Tutoring is offered in any math or science course, from college prep all the way up to AP level.

From the program's original pitch, 2016:
Starting in the 2015–2016 school year, the tutoring program was an immediate success among students and parents. Since then, MSJ STEM Success has served over 200 students across 20+ subjects, while expanding its core offerings every year.

How we're run

Student leadership. Teacher supervision.

01

Student-led

A rotating leadership team of MSJ students recruit tutor, match pairs, and run day-to-day operations.

02

Math & science dept.

The program operates under the MSJHS math and science department, with classroom space and teacher oversight.

03

Peer-taught

Every tutor has already completed, and done well in, the exact course they're teaching.
