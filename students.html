For students

Get matched with a tutor who's already done well in your course.

The process

  1. Sign up online

    Fill out the sign-up form with the subject(s) you'd like help in.

  2. Wait for a match

    A qualified tutor will contact you within a few weeks. If every spot is full, you'll be placed on a waitlist and notified as soon as one opens.

  3. Pick a time slot

    Once you're matched, you and your tutor find a rhythm that works for both of you.

  4. Keep meeting

    You'll work with the same tutor for a semester or more, in any of the subjects you requested.

Where & when

Sessions run in MSJ classrooms, teacher-supervised.

Meetings happen after school or during Advisory, 2 days a week. Your tutor will confirm the exact classroom and time once you're matched. You can also check the tentative schedule for each month to see the available days.

Commitment agreement

We'd love to serve everyone — here's why spots are protected.

Because tutor capacity is limited and there's usually a waitlist, we ask every student who signs up to agree to two things:

  • Meet with your tutor at least twice a month — or risk forfeiting your spot to a student on the waitlist.
  • Show up to scheduled meetings, or give your tutor notice if you have a conflict. A repeated pattern of no-shows also risks forfeiting your spot.

Ready to get matched?

The sign-up form takes about two minutes. Bring your class name and, if you can, a couple of times that usually work for you.
